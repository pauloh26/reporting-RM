Definitions & Data Sources
Traffic
Organic engaged sessions from GA4, exported by country (download.csv) and by page (download__1_.csv). Paid traffic excluded.
Conversion
The engaged-sessions value from the GA4 "Organic conversions" exports (download__2_.csv / download__3_.csv), treated as conversions per the report owner's instruction. Not BigQuery trials.
Conv. Rate
Conversions ÷ Traffic, computed from period totals (not an average of per-row rates). Shown to two decimals.
Focus Region
Countries mapped to Rentman's canonical regions. Mexico → Other; Luxembourg → DACH+L; Nordics incl. Iceland → Scandinavia.
Locale Homepage
Root path per locale: / (EN), /nl, /de, /fr, /es, /it.
WoW
Current week vs the immediately preceding week. No year-over-year basis in this report.

What the Numbers Say
↓Broad-based softeningTraffic −6.5% · Conv −19.2%
Both traffic and conversions fell this week, with conversions dropping roughly 3× faster than traffic. Blended conversion rate slipped from 8.52% to 7.37%. Nearly every region declined, so this looks like a week-level dip rather than one region dragging the total.
↑North AmericaTraffic +8.3%
The only large region to grow traffic (564→611), driven by the US (+10.2%). Conversions still eased slightly (37→33), so the conv. rate softened — more visitors, marginally fewer converting. Worth watching whether next week confirms the traffic gain.
→"Other" carries conversion87 of 160 conv
The Other catch-all is the single biggest conversion bucket (~54% of all conversions) at a 14.45% rate — far above any focus region. This concentration is a blind spot: we can't see which countries inside Other drive it. A country-level drill-down on Other would be the highest-value follow-up.
↓ItalyTraffic −39.7%
Italy fell hardest on traffic (116→70) and the /it homepage halved (84→47). Conversions dropped 7→2. Small base means noise, but the size of the traffic drop is notable — worth a quick check for a ranking loss or a one-off spike in the prior week.
↑Spanish homepageConv +100%
Flat traffic on /es (126→126) but conversions doubled (6→12) — the standout converter among locale homepages this week. Base is small so treat as a hypothesis, but if it holds it's a positive signal for the Iberia homepage.
↓/pricing−39 sessions
The biggest single-page traffic loss (195→156, −20%). Pricing is a high-intent page, so a sustained decline here could pressure conversions downstream. Check whether this is seasonal or a search-visibility change.
